The Founder

Kyle Sincock
Founder, CycleLead LLC

I started CycleLead because I kept seeing the same problem with small businesses: they were pouring money into ads and lead generation while completely ignoring the most valuable asset they already had — a list of people who had already shown interest.

The fix isn't complicated. A short, personal email — written like it came from the owner, not a marketing department — sent to the right people at the right time. No graphics, no unsubscribe banners, no corporate tone. Just a real message that reminds someone why they liked this business in the first place.

I built CycleLead around a performance model because I believe in the work. If I'm not recovering revenue for you, I shouldn't be getting paid. It's that simple.

Why It Works

The Plain-Text Advantage

Corporate-looking email blasts get ignored. Personal-looking emails get read. Here's why our approach consistently outperforms traditional email marketing.

📬

Inbox Placement

Plain-text emails avoid the Promotions tab and spam filters that catch HTML marketing blasts. They land in the primary inbox — where people actually read.

🤝

Warm Relationships

These aren't cold strangers. They've already interacted with your business. A familiar name in their inbox has a completely different effect than an ad.

✍️

Written in Your Voice

Every email reads like it came directly from the business owner — casual, specific, human. Readers don't feel marketed to. They feel like someone remembered them.

🎯

Micro-Segmentation

We group contacts by behavior and send targeted copy to each segment — past customers and old inquiries get completely different angles and messaging.

How We Operate

A Few Things We Won't Compromise On

Deliverability First
We never send a single email before the list is cleaned and the sending infrastructure is properly authenticated. Protecting your domain reputation is non-negotiable.
Full Transparency
You see every lead we generate. At the end of the campaign, we go through the numbers together and agree on what closed. No black box, no guessing.
Your Data Stays Yours
We never sell, share, or retain your customer data beyond the campaign window. Everything is handled under a signed agreement before we touch anything.
Performance Only
No retainers. No setup fees. Our fee is 15% of what we actually close for you — and only after it closes.
