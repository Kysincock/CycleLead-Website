How It Works

From old spreadsheet to
new booking in four steps

01

You hand us your list

Export your old contacts from wherever they live — spreadsheet, CRM, booking software, even a notes file. We accept any format and handle everything from there.

02

We clean and verify every address

We run your entire list through NeverBounce to remove dead, invalid, and bounced addresses. You pay the verification cost at $8 per 1,000 emails — passed through at cost, zero markup — plus a flat $25 to set up your dedicated sending domain.

03

We write and send the campaign

We segment your list and write a short, personal email sequence in your voice — no corporate blasts, no HTML templates. Emails read like a note from you personally, which is exactly why they get responses.

04

You get bookings — we collect our share

Hot replies come straight to your inbox. We track every lead, and at the end of the month we review closed appointments together. You pay 15% of what actually closed. Not a penny more, not a penny before.
